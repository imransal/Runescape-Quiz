Quiz Instructions

  1. Begin the Quiz
  2. Each round consists of 10 questions, and you must select one answer for each question.
  3. When you answer a question correctly, the answer will turn green.
  4. When you answer a question incorrectly, you will hear a different sound, and the answer will turn red.
  5. Your total score will be displayed in the top right corner of the screen.
  6. The question number you are on will be displayed in the top left corner of the screen.
  7. After answering all the questions, you will be directed to the end page where you can save your score to the leaderbaord to track your progress and improvement over time.
